Our Team

As mentionned before, our robotics club started participating in the CRC competiton in 2011. At that time, there were only three members to the team. But, over the years, this number has grown. It has now reached nineteen members. Every member tries to help with their abilities or abilities that they can learn. We all try our best and do what we can.

The team is composed of many sub-groups that are responsible for a specific part in the competition. For example, we have a kiosk team, a video team, a website team, and a robot team. But, since we don't have that many members, some people are in more than one sub-group at a time. These are then managed by the co-captains. They help and direct the teammates.

The CRC competitions are developing students' knowledge every year. This year, our robotics club has learned many things. But, we mainly learned robot building and programming. Not only this, but we also developped our coding skills. We always look forward to it. We always look forward to learing new things every year, and it is the thing that we enjoy the most. For the Flip game, our team, really enjoyed making our themed kiosk and learning more about robots. Even though it was difficult at times since we lacked knowlege, we still enjoyed it. Also, we often problems with the robot. It is the only aspect from this competition that we really struggled with. But, in order to overcome this, we needed to make sacrifices. We sometimes skipped some classes or came to school on pedagogical days. We also stayed after school more often as the competition approached. When there was a month left, for example, we stayed after school every day of the week.
