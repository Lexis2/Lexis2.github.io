Our School
Our school, Lake of Two Mountains High School, is located at 2105 Guy in Two-Mountains. In 1983, this ‘new’ school replaced the former building at 203 14th avenue where many problems occured. Because we count … students, it creates a feeling of being part of a large family. Everybody knows each other. Clubs and activities highlight this as well. These include the cheerleading team, the basketball team, the volleyball team, the robotics club, woodworking shop, the yearbook committee, student leaders, and more.

LTM, is also a place where teachers are very included. They will try to find time to help you as much as possible; they will laugh with you and try to make you feel better. Our Robotics club mentor, for example, is a science teacher. Mr. Truesdale. He tries to find time — which he doesn’t always have — for us to be able to be included in this amazing club. This person is not the only person to get out of their way to do this.
