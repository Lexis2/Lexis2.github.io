Our School
LTM's History...
Lake of Two Mountains High School, which is located at 2105 Guy Street in the town of Two-Mountains, was build in 1983; it replaced the old building with the same name which was located at 203 14th Avenue. Here, the first class graduated in 1954.

This high school now has approximately 300 students. They all come from a variety of towns: Two-Mountains, St-Eustache, Sainte-Marthe-sur-le-Lac, St-Joseph, Oka, Mirabel, and even more.

Clubs and Activities...
In our school, there are many clubs and activities that take place. Clubs such as, the woodworking club, the robotics club, the yearbook committee, the student leaders, the mindfulness club, are enjoyed by students of all ages; many of them learn new skills with these. There are a lot of sports teams in the school as well. These include the basketball team, the futsall team, the hockey team, the softball team, and much more.

When it comes to activities, there are many organized by the school. A Europe and New York City trip is organized for seniors where they can visit new places and even learn about new cultures. Smaller activities are also organized such as skating outings, skiing and snowboarding outings, and even our yearly Color Clash event, where the whole school competes in various games.
